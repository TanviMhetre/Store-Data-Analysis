# Store-Data-Analysis
# Vrinda Store Sales Analysis (Excel Dashboard Project)
This project analyzes Vrinda Store’s 2022 sales data to identify key revenue drivers, customer behavior patterns, and opportunities to improve sales performance. An interactive Excel dashboard was built to enable quick and data-driven business decisions.

# Key Business Questions
1. Which month had the highest sales and order volume?
2. Who contributes more to sales: men or women?
3. What are the major order statuses?
4. Which states generate the highest revenue?
5. How do age and gender impact purchasing behavior?
6. Which sales channels and product categories drive maximum revenue?

# Key Insights
1. Women contributed ~65% of total purchases, indicating a strong female customer base.
2. Customers aged 30–49 years contributed ~50% of total orders, making them the most valuable segment.
3. Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh together contributed ~35% of total sales.
4. E-commerce platforms (Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra) accounted for ~80% of total revenue.

# Business Recommendations
1. Focus marketing efforts on women aged 30–49 years to maximize conversion.
2. Prioritize high-performing states for targeted campaigns and inventory planning.
3. Leverage e-commerce platforms for promotions, discounts, and customer acquisition.
4. Align product strategy with top-performing categories and peak sales periods.

# Outcome
Developed a user-friendly dashboard that helps identify high-value customers, top-performing regions, and effective sales channels, enabling better strategic decision-making.
